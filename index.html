About us

Nova Information Security specializes in providing comprehensive security consulting services. Founded on September 5, 2023, Nova is dedicated to enhancing the cybersecurity posture of businesses through a range of services including web application penetration tests, network penetration tests, code review and bug bounty triage. Operating out of San Francisco, Nova aims to address the critical need for robust security measures in today’s digital landscape. The company is lead by Denis Smajlović, a seasoned security professional with extensive experience in the field. Nova distinguishes itself by offering personalized consulting services, focusing on identifying and remedying security vulnerabilities in applications, CRM systems, and advising on securing devices on corporate networks. With a unique approach that combines technical prowess with a deep understanding of the compliance requirements facing financial institutions and other businesses, Nova is poised to become a trusted partner for companies looking to safeguard their digital assets.
